    Operational Attributes

    Operational Attributes are implicit attributes created by TokenScript Engine

    Operational Attributes are implicit attributes created by TokenScript Engine. They are created for Tokens as well as Data Objects.

    For Ethereum tokens, the most important Operational Attribute is ownerAddress, whose value is the Ethereum address of the token's owner.

    Other operational attributes being proposed (probably will be included in future TokenScript specifictions) includes:
    • createTimestamp
    • createBlockHeight
    • receiveTimeStamp
